About Us

WHO WE ARE

Muhammad Iftikhar started Saim Electric in 1999 with one goal: do electrical work the right way — safe, clean, and built to last.

Over 25 years, we've wired hundreds of homes, apartments, and offices across Karachi — from DHA bungalows to Clifton commercial towers. Our work is built on three things: safety first, quality materials, and honest pricing.

Today we offer more than just wiring. We install CCTV systems, set up computer networks, connect smart home devices, and handle generator & solar setups — all under one roof.
